Ukrainian Folklore

Fairy Tales


Ukrainian folk fairy tales is a popular genre of Ukrainian traditional folk art.

These stories, which have been handed down from generation to generation, embody wisdom of the ancestors, their life experience, feelings and dreams.

The main idea of any folk fairy tale is that a good, kind, brave and honest person with an open heart always overcomes all obstacles and gains victory over evil forces.
